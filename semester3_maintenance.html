Post-Beta Bug Triage

Systematically processing all bugs reported by beta testers

After beta testing with 8–10 users, you'll have a list of reported issues. Triage means reviewing each issue, determining its severity, and deciding: Fix now / Fix in Sem 4 / Won't fix.

Fix P1 and P2 Before Production

All P1 (blocker) and P2 (major) bugs must be fixed before the Semester 4 production deployment. P3 (minor) bugs can be deferred to the Semester 4 maintenance phase. Never deploy to production with an open P1 bug.
