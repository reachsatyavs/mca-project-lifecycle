Customer Features

F-01 through F-08 — core customer-facing capabilities

F-01 Customer Registration High Sprint 1 ↗
Allow new customers to create an account using name, email, phone, and password. Email verification is sent; account is inactive until verified. Duplicate emails are rejected.
Traces to US-C01 | UC-01 Register Account | FR-101 | Test Cases TC-01 TC-02
F-02 Customer Login & Authentication High Sprint 1 ↗
Allow registered customers to log in with email and password. System issues JWT access token (15 min) + refresh token (7 days). Session persists across page reloads. Invalid credentials show a generic error with no hint about which field is wrong (security).
Traces to US-C02 | UC-02 Login | FR-102 | Test Cases TC-01 TC-02 SEC-01 SEC-05
F-03 Restaurant Search & Browse High Sprint 2 ↗
Customers can search restaurants by name or filter by cuisine type, rating, and price range. Results appear within 2 seconds and show restaurant name, rating, distance, and estimated delivery time. No results shows a friendly message.
Traces to US-C03 | UC-05 UC-06 | FR-301 | Test Cases TC-11 TC-12 PERF-02
F-04 Cart Management High Sprint 3
Customers can add items, update quantities, and remove items from a cart. Cart is single-restaurant and persists across page refreshes. Adding items from a second restaurant triggers a "Clear cart?" warning dialog.
Traces to US-C04 | UC-07 | FR-302 | Test Cases TC-21 TC-22
F-05 Online Payment & Checkout High Sprint 3
Customer selects delivery address and payment method (Credit/Debit Card, UPI, or COD), confirms order. Payment is processed via Razorpay sandbox. Success creates order; failure shows error with retry — cart is not cleared. Invoice PDF is generated.
Traces to US-C05 | UC-08 Place Order | FR-303 · FR-401 · FR-402 | Test Cases TC-04 TC-05 TC-06 PERF-04
F-06 Real-Time Order Tracking High Sprint 4
After placing an order, customers see a visual timeline: Placed → Accepted → Preparing → Out for Delivery → Delivered. Status auto-refreshes every 15 seconds. Push/email notification sent on each status change.
Traces to US-C06 | UC-09 | FR-304 | Test Cases TC-36 TC-37 SEC-04
F-07 Rating & Review Medium Sprint 5
After delivery, customers can rate the restaurant (1–5 stars) and add an optional text comment (max 500 characters). Only one review per order. Restaurant average rating recalculated within 1 minute.
Traces to US-C07 | UC-12 Write Review | FR-601 | Test Cases TC-08 TC-09 TC-10 SEC-02
F-08 Order History & Reorder Medium Sprint 5
Customers can view all past orders sorted by date (newest first), see itemised details, and reorder a previous meal in one click. Full invoice/receipt downloadable as PDF.
Traces to US-C08 | UC-11 | FR-305 · FR-403 | Test Cases TC-48 SEC-04

Restaurant Owner Features

F-09 through F-11

F-09 Restaurant Registration & Approval High Sprint 2 ↗
Restaurant owners register with name, address, cuisine type, opening hours, and FSSAI licence number. Registration requires admin approval before the restaurant becomes visible to customers. Owner is notified by email on approval or rejection.
Traces to US-O01 | UC-13 | FR-201 | Test Cases TC-31 TC-32
F-10 Menu Management High Sprint 2 ↗
Restaurant owners can add, edit, and delete menu items. Each item has a name, description, price, category, photo, and veg/non-veg flag. Items can be toggled available/unavailable. Changes reflect in customer menu within 30 seconds.
Traces to US-O02 | UC-14 | FR-202 | Test Cases TC-33 TC-34 SEC-03
F-11 Order Management (Restaurant Side) High Sprint 3
New orders trigger a visual and audio notification on the owner dashboard. Owner can Accept or Reject within 5 minutes (auto-reject on timeout). Accepted orders display items, quantity, delivery address, and payment mode.
Traces to US-O03 | UC-15 | FR-203 | Test Cases TC-35
🛵

Delivery Partner Features

F-12 through F-13

F-12 Partner Online / Offline Toggle Medium Sprint 4
Delivery partners can toggle their availability. When offline, no orders are assigned. Going offline is blocked during an active delivery until the order is delivered.
Traces to US-P01 | UC-16 | FR-501 | Test Cases TC-41
F-13 Order Delivery Flow High Sprint 4
Partner portal shows assigned orders with restaurant and customer details. Partner marks order as "Picked Up" and "Delivered". Each status change sends push/email notifications to the customer.
Traces to US-P02 | UC-17 | FR-502 · FR-503 | Test Cases TC-42 TC-43

Admin Features

F-14 through F-16

F-14 Restaurant Approval (Admin) High Sprint 1 ↗
Admin reviews pending restaurant applications, inspects submitted details, and approves or rejects with a reason. Restaurant owner is notified by email on approval or rejection.
Traces to US-A01 | UC-18 | FR-602 | Test Cases TC-51 TC-52 SEC-03
F-15 Platform Analytics Dashboard Medium Sprint 5
Admin views platform-wide statistics: total orders today, total revenue, active restaurants, and active users. Trend charts show daily order volume for the past 30 days. Admin can filter by date range.
Traces to US-A02 | UC-19 | FR-602 | Test Cases TC-55 PERF-03
F-16 User Suspension / Ban Medium Sprint 5
Admin can search for any user by name or email, and suspend (temporary) or ban (permanent) them with a reason. Suspended users see a clear message on login. Suspensions can be reversed by admin.
Traces to US-A03 | UC-20 | FR-602 | Test Cases TC-56 TC-57 SEC-03

Key Rule — No Orphan Features

Every feature must have at least one user story, one use case, and one test case linked to it. A feature with no test case is untested scope. A test case with no feature is unplanned scope creep — both are red flags in a professional project review.
